About This Project

The Quantum Harmonic Oscillator (QHO) has exact ground state energy E₀ = ½ℏω. This project uses the Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) with IBM's qiskit-nature library to recover this energy computationally and validates it against the exact analytical result.
